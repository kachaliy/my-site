SMS Consent Policy

This page explains how SMS consent is gathered by Alex Kachaliy for personal and professional communication purposes.

Consent to receive SMS messages is collected through the following methods:

Opt-Out

At any time, you may opt out of receiving SMS messages by replying STOP. For more information, reply with HELP.
